Overview

In this all-new mode, we're breaking from the usual format of red vs blue and increasing the number of teams to five!
Each team has their own spawn point on the map,
and there are stage events that occur at certain intervals.

Gameplay

Five teams face off against each other on the battlefield. Points are gained by defeating heroes from the other teams.
The game timer is set at 8 minutes. Once the 8 minutes are up, teams are ranked according to their scores and displayed from 1st to 5th.
Each Death Match team only consists of two players, so a high level of cooperation between teammates is required for victory.
